Salah: Our daily meeting with the King of Kings, our Creator and a reaffirmation of our servitude. Salah is the second pillar of Islam and the foundation around which a believers life is built. Salah is the key to attaining success in this life in the Dunya. Whenever the Prophet faced any difficulty, he turned to Allah in Salah. Salah is the first thing we as Muslims will be asked about on the Day of Judgement. If something is so important for our stay in this world as well as the hereafter, then it should be of utmost importance for us to learn about it and make sure that we are doing it correctly. 

Our beloved Prophet ﷺ, commanded us, "Pray as you see me pray". This teaches us that Allah will accept only that prayer which is in line and in accordance to the Sunnah taught by His Messenger ﷺ. Sifat al-Salatun-Nabi is a renowned work of the late Imam and scholar of Hadeeth, Sheikh Muhammad Nasirudin Al-Albani. The original work in Arabic was a unique milestone with a fresh approach in presenting the topic. We are proud to present before the reader, the new abridged English version of it. The intent behind the compilation of the book was clearly noted by the author, and he stated, "I did not come across a book (on prayer) that covered the topic comprehensively, so I felt obliged to produce a book that collected together as many features of the Prophet's prayer (peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) as possible, from the takbeer (opening of the prayer) to the tasleem (concluding of the prayer)..."
